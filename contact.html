Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with BilzCrew

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple websites take 4-6 weeks, while complex applications may take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing options including fixed-price packages for defined scopes, hourly rates for ongoing work, and retainer agreements for long-term partnerships. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! All our projects include a 30-day warranty period, and we offer various support packages including 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, and dedicated account management.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We often collaborate with in-house teams, providing expertise and additional resources as needed. We can integrate seamlessly with your existing workflows and tools.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience across various industries including e-commerce, healthcare, finance, education, and technology. Our adaptable approach allows us to deliver value regardless of your industry.

How do we get started?

Simply fill out the contact form or email us at hello@bilzcrew.com. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs and provide a detailed proposal.
